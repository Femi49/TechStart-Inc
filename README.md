### TechStart Inc
**ISO 27001 Risk Assessment for Cloud Migration**

![Progress](https://img.shields.io/badge/Progress-100%25-success) ![ISO 27001](https://img.shields.io/badge/-ISO%2027001-informational) ![NIST CSF](https://img.shields.io/badge/-NIST%20CSF-informational) 

| Assets | Risks | Controls | Tasks |
|--------|-------|----------|-------|
| 5 | 5 | 8 | 4/4 |

**Context:** The Companys existing on -premises infrastructure presents several constraints:

1. Scalability limitations: Difficulty handling increasing customer volume and enterprise workloads

2. Operational inefficiencies: High maintenance for hardware, backups and disaster recovery

3. Limited resilience: Absence of roust disaster recovery .

WHY THIS PROJECT WAS NEEDED
1. To meet Enterprise Client Requirements
2.To achieve  ISO 27001:2022 Certification
3. To increase Security Posture
4. To Reduce Long-Term Operational Costs
5. To Strengthen Business Continuity & Resilience
6. To Enable Scalability and Performance


**Key Accomplishments:**
- Conducted comprehensive asset inventory and classification for 5 information assets supporting ISO 27001 ISMS implementation
- Performed risk assessment identifying 5 risks across cloud migration scope, including 1 critical and 4 high-severity risks requiring immediate attention
- Implemented 8 security controls aligned with ISO 27001 Annex A requirements and NIST CSF
- Developed 5 risk treatment plans with documented mitigation strategies, acceptance criteria, and residual risk calculations
- Completed ISO 27001 risk assessment documentation supporting certification readiness for cloud infrastructure

**Decisions Made:** 1. The usage of a Managed Database Service: It has a built-in encryption, backups and high availablility

2. Enforced Encryption (At Rest and In Transit): This decision protecs sensitive customer data (PII,billing), and also mitigates exposure during interception.

3. Implemented Least Privilege IAM Model: This reduces risk of privilege escalation and insider threats

4. Perform Backup Validation and DR Testing: This ensures backups are actually restorable, and also reduces the risk of data loss. It is required for business continuity assurance.

**Outcomes:** 1. Significant reduction in high and critical risks identified during mitigation

2. Strong improvement in identity governance and privileged access control

3.Cloud Security Posture Improvement

4. Data Protection & Encryption
